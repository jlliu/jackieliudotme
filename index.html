hi my name is jackie!

I'm a junior at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology studying computer science and design. I make things out of (but not limited to) paint, code, and tears.

Previously, I've made software working as a front-end web engineer at Formlabs and as an undergraduate researcher in the MIT Media Lab.

I love exploring the blurry intersection of art and technology, and am particularly interested in themes of human intimacy, personal expression, and social change.

Other interests include gender studies, critical design, harmonious color palettes, candid coming-of-age narratives about girlhood, and iced lattes.
